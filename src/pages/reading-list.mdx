I enjoy learning from books and articles more than video content, so I made this list to keep track of what I've read.

All books are sorted by publication date.

## Must read

Here's stuff that I believe every software engineer, no matter their specialization, will find of great value to know.

- **The Mythical Man-Month** by Fred Brooks (ISBN: `978-0201835953`) - 1995
- **Peopleware: Productive Projects and Teams** by Tom DeMarco, Tim Lister (ISBN: `978-0321934116`) - 2016
- **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** by Martin Kleppmann (ISBN: `978-1449373320`) - 2017
- **Real-World Cryptography** by David Wong (ISBN: `978-1617296710`) - 2021

## Recommended

Great reads, if you're interested in the topics covered.

- **Domain-Driven Design: Tackling Complexity in the Heart of Software** by Eric Evans (ISBN: `978-0321125217`) - 2003
- [Site Reliability Engineering](https://sre.google/sre-book/table-of-contents/) by Betsy Beyer, Chris Jones, Jennifer Petoff, Niall Richard Murphy - 2016
- [Building Secure & Reliable Systems](https://google.github.io/building-secure-and-reliable-systems/raw/toc.html) by Heather Adkins, Betsy Beyer, Paul Blankinship, Ana Oprea, Piotr Lewandowski, Adam Stubblefield (ISBN: `978-1492083122`) - 2020
- **Understanding Distributed Systems** by Roberto Vitillo (ISBN: `978-1838430207`) - 2021
- **Foundations of Scalable Systems** by Ian Gorton (ISBN: `978-1098106065`) - 2022
